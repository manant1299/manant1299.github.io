Plant Propagation

Propagation is about Cuttings, growing plants form seeds and more thing about plants

Plant propagation is process of growing new plants form different sources

Growing plants from seed uses sexaul reproduction because both male and female parts of the flower interact to form a seed. This seed is genetically unique.

how to grow plants from seeds

  • 1. Choose a container that is suitable most be big enough for the roots to grow and have holes to drain.
  • 2. Make sure the container washed and sterilise.
  • 3. Make sure that the seed is fresh check if it needs special conditions.
  • 4. Fill the container with seed mix.
  • 5. Level the mix to the rim of the container.
  • 6. Pack closely the mix gently using something to flatten it.
  • 7. Check the packed to see how depth and how spread parted.
  • 8. Lightly water the seed.
  • 9. Label the plants with what they are.
  • 10. Put the container in a warm place.
